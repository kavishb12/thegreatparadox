Dear Diary Section

Today is the first day of the new fitness schedule. I started with a happy mood, so happy that I overslept by over 30 mins! Not a good start to the day. I wanted everything to be in the perfect order today. But as life goes, whenever you want something to happen properly, it never does. Anyway, today was legs day. My legs are the weakest muscle in my body. This has to be expected as I would prefer sharing an ice-cream with the great trump than training my legs. Before this, I used to train my legs only once a month. So with my new exercise routine, I was expecting that my legs would definitely be soaring with pain. Luckily, this is not the case. My legs aren't hurting that much. I hope I did the exercises in the right way.

Goodbye for now. See you in the next blog!
